Ternion Intelligence

Client
Ternion Intelligence
Involvement
1 Product Lead · 1 Brand Designer · 1 Interaction Designer · 1 Front-End Engineer
Scope
Branding package · Executive landing page · Brochure
Results
A poised, data-rich presence that turns complex power analysis into clear executive takeaway.
GOAL

Ternion analyses geopolitical risk through a triple lens—past, present, possible—yet had only a placeholder page. They sought a brand that spoke power without panic and a landing that hinted at live analysis without exposing secure data.

RESULTS

A triskelion knot—three arms spiralling into a focused core—anchors the identity. Deep navy holds the page steady while electric-teal strokes signal emerging threats. Line animations trace silent data flows behind stark grids, suggesting constant intake without noise. Content unfolds in three beats—what Ternion sees, how it thinks, why it matters—mirroring the firm’s teleological method and leaving visitors with a cool sense of precision and trust.
