SMARTER.AI – THE AGENTIC HIRING CONSOLE

Recruiters once juggled calendars, CRMs, and analytics across ten windows. We folded the journey into a single glass-layered console. Every module follows context. A library of modular cards listens to user context. Open a candidate, and availability, interview slots, and AI notes surface in lockstep. Pivot to pipeline health, and the same chassis reshapes into gap analysis and time-to-hire trends. The console feels conversational: an on-screen coach suggests next steps and drafts outreach in plain language. Smarter's team launched the new flow in a single sprint; recruiter focus climbed and candidate drop-off fell.

CYRCL — SOCIAL NAVIGATION IN ONE SWIPE

Cyrcl asked for an app that treats presence as the only metric. We centred everything under the thumb. One radial menu fans out voice, photo, and follow-up in a single sweep, so navigation never pulls attention away from conversation. Posts remain circular, echoing the brand mark and removing hierarchy from the feed. Subtle haptics confirm intent, keeping the surface calm. Early users moved through the app instinctively; feedback called the experience "as natural as turning a wrist."

SIGNALBOARD — INSIGHT AT FIRST GLANCE

Ops leaders stared at dashboards yet still hunted for meaning. We built a canvas that prioritises action over display. Teams drag a metric onto the grid, and it bonds to live data on contact. Role blueprints load views that feel hand-picked for sales, support, or finance without extra clicks. Colour tokens let each client re-skin the suite in minutes, so brand and clarity coexist. Executives explored real numbers during our first review call; their questions shifted from "where is the data?" to "what do we do next?"
