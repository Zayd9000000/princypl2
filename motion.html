Visual and Motion Design

Pixels that move purposefully.
Great motion is a function in disguise—it guides attention, explains state changes, and makes your brand feel alive without stealing a millisecond of load time. Our Visual & Motion track treats animation like any other product feature: storyboarded against user goals, optimized for GPU cost, and handed off as reusable assets engineers can drop straight into prod. From lightweight Lottie files to full-scene WebGL, every deliverable ships with performance budgets, fallback states, and a single source of truth in both Figma and code.
