Improving Contractors' Experience with a New IoT Mobile App Design.

Client
Toughbuilt
Involvement
2 Dedicated UI/UX Designers
Scope
Mobile App, Web App, E-commerce
Results
Our collaboration with ToughBuilt resulted in the development of a the mobile app that serves as a digital companion to their physical devices. By creating a seamless integration between the app and ToughBuilt's products, we set their offering apart from competitors, leading to a notable increase in sales.
GOAL

Streamline contractors' workflow with a device management app.

Contractors face the ongoing challenge of managing their projects efficiently while juggling various tools and applications. The cluttered and disorganized nature of traditional workflows often hampers productivity and creates frustration. ToughBuilt recognized this pain point and approached us with a clear mission to transform the contractor's workflow.

Our goal was to create an app that simplifies daily tasks and consolidates all project data into a single, intuitive platform. By designing a user-friendly interface and leveraging innovative technology, we enabled contractors to streamline their workflows and manage projects seamlessly.

RESULTS

A digital companion for the physical laser device

The mobile app simplifies the contractor's experience by allowing them to interact with physical devices, link measurements with notes and pictures, and effortlessly oversee their projects. Through the power of design and technology, we successfully addressed the challenges faced by contractors, making their daily tasks easier and more efficient.

Our partnership with ToughBuilt exemplifies our commitment to delivering transformative design solutions that empower businesses and enhance user experiences.  It highlights the value of design and technology in solving real-world challenges faced by professionals in the construction industry.
