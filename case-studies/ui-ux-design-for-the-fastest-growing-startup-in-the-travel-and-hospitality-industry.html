GOAL

PrFrom Self Check-In to room TV app - elevating the traveling experience.

We have collaborated with Life House on multiple design projects, all with a shared goal: to offer young travelers a refreshing alternative to traditional hotels by creating user-friendly design solutions.

We focused on optimizing user experience, spanning from streamlining the check-in process with one-click functionality to seamless communication with the management team through mobile and room TV applications.

RESULTS

Collaboration over years.

It brings us great joy to mention that our design director, Olha, was a founding designer at Life House. Her expertise and innovative design solutions have been instrumental in the company's success and have played a significant role in securing substantial funding from notable investors, amounting to tens of millions of dollars.

Over the course of the past five years, we have collaborated on various projects ranging from designing intuitive UI/UX interfaces for TV applications and self-checking kiosks to crafting captivating and user-friendly hotel websites. As a trusted partner, we are thrilled to be a part of Life House's journey, continually contributing thoughtful design solutions to elevate their brand and create exceptional experiences for their guests.
